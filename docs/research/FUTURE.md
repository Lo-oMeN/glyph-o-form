## Community Vision

### Who We Are

The Glyph-o-betics community isn't a market. It's a **commons**.

We're:
- **Learners** seeking new ways to understand
- **Teachers** creating inclusive classrooms
- **Makers** building tools for human flourishing
- **Dreamers** imagining better ways to communicate
- **Guardians** protecting attention and autonomy

### How We Work

**Open Source, Always**
- All code MIT licensed
- All specifications public
- All research open access
- No proprietary lock-in, ever

**Decentralized Governance**
- Community-elected steering committee
- Working groups for specific domains (education, accessibility, etc.)
- Rough consensus and running code
- Fork-friendly: if you disagree, take the code and build your vision

**Sustainable Economics**
- Grants for research and accessibility
- Commercial licenses for enterprise (funds the commons)
- Donations from individual supporters
- No venture capital, no growth-at-all-costs, no exits

### The Culture We Build

**Kindness Over Cleverness**
Anyone can write clever code. We write kind code—accessible, documented, welcoming to newcomers.

**Depth Over Speed**
We resist the pressure to ship fast and break things. We ship carefully and build things that last.

**Inclusion as Practice**
Not a value we put on the website. A practice we embed in every decision. Who isn't at the table? Whose needs aren't being met?

**Play as Method**
Serious work doesn't have to be grim. We experiment, we prototype, we laugh at our failures and learn.
